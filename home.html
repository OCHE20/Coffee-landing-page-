Our Menu

EXPRESSO
Rich, dark and full-bodied

Expresso coffee is a concentrated, rich coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-grounded coffee beans at high pressure. Known for its intense flavor and creamy layer of crema on top, expresso the foundation for many coffee drinks like lattes, cappuccinos, and macchiatos. Originating in Italy, expresso highlights deep, bold flavors in a small, potent shot. Its quick preparation for strong taste have made it a popular choice worldwide, especially for those who enjoy a quick, energizing coffee experience.

CAPPUCCINOS
Perfect blend of coffee and frothy milk

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, creating a balanced blend of bold coffee flavor and creamy texture. Traditionally served in a small cup, cappuccinos are known for their rich, velvety taste and distinct layers, with the milk foam often topped with a sprinkle of cocoa powder or cinnamon. This drink is loved for its smooth mouthfeel and slightly sweet, frothy finish, making it a popular choice for coffee enthusiasts seeking a harmonious espresso-based beverage.

LATTE
Smooth and creamy with a hint of expresso

A latte is a popular espresso-based coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and topped with steamed milk, creating a creamy, smooth texture with a mild coffee flavor. Typically served in a larger cup, a latte has more milk than a cappuccino, making it less intense and more approachable for those who enjoy a balanced, mellow coffee taste.
